02 — CASE STUDIESPROBLEM · SOLUTION · IMPACT

CASE
STUDIES.

IEE — Integrated Employment Ecosystem.

Founder · Full product ownership
Problem

India's employment ecosystem is fragmented — scattered job portals, unverifiable skill claims, disconnected government schemes, no unified worker identity. Students, workers, employers, and institutions all operate in silos with no trusted layer between them.

Solution

A unified digital platform built on the principle "One Labour · One Identity" — a verified employment passport, skill passport, AI smart-hiring, digital labour chowk, QR verification, workforce analytics, and integration with government schemes, connecting eight participant ecosystems under one trusted layer.

Impact

Designed to serve students, skilled workers, contractors, employers, institutions, skill orgs, and government departments — with the long-term goal of becoming India's digital employment infrastructure and enabling verified international employment pathways.

10 core modules · 8+ ecosystems · 2025 — ongoing

Leader Connect — Hyperlocal Community.

Product Builder · Under FORGE
Problem

Local political and civic engagement happens in fragmented WhatsApp groups and generic social feeds — no verified leader identity, no hyperlocal targeting down to village level, no trustworthy channel for constituency-level updates and creator content.

Solution

A secure, mobile-first platform with OTP auth, verified leader profiles, hyperlocal feeds scoped by district / constituency / mandal / village, community circles, AI-assisted poster and video generation, multi-language support, and a subscription creative studio.

Impact

Delivered as a scalable ecosystem for local leaders, communities, and creators — a foundation for civic engagement, content moderation, and content monetization built for India-scale mobile use.

12+ features · 4 location tiers · shipped under FORGE
